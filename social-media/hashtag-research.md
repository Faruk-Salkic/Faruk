# Hashtag Strategy by Platform

## Instagram
| Type | Size | Count |
|------|------|-------|
| Niche specific | 10k-100k posts | 5-7 |
| Topic broad | 100k-1M posts | 3-5 |
| Brand or campaign | Any | 1-2 |

Total: 10-15 per post
Avoid: like4like, follow4follow, instagood — shadow ban risk

## LinkedIn
- Use 3-5 max
- Niche relevant only: digitalmarketing, googleads, seo
- Broad tags like business, entrepreneur have low reach now

## TikTok
- fyp and foryou still help slightly
- Add 3-5 niche tags
- Check Discover tab weekly for trending tags in your niche

## Research Process
1. Search your main keyword on Instagram
2. Check Related Hashtags section
3. Avoid anything over 5M posts — too crowded
4. Check if top posts are high quality — signals active community
5. Save good tags in a notes doc by category
