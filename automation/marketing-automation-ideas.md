# Marketing Automation Ideas

## Lead Management
- Lead scoring on form submit based on role, company, source
- CRM enrichment via Clearbit or Apollo when lead is created
- Follow-up trigger: if lead opens email 3x without booking, alert sales in Slack
- Dead lead reactivation: tag 60-day inactive leads, add to re-engagement campaign

## Content and Social
- Blog to social: new post published, auto-draft LinkedIn + Instagram caption via AI
- RSS digest: collect industry news daily, format, post to team Slack at 8am
- Repurpose machine: long post to 5 tweet variants + 1 LinkedIn post via AI
- Brand mention monitor: watch Reddit and Twitter mentions, alert in Slack

## Reporting
- Weekly GA4 digest: pull key metrics, format email, send to client every Monday
- Ad spend alert: if daily Google Ads spend exceeds threshold, send SMS
- Rank tracker: check keyword positions weekly, flag drops of 5+ positions

## Client Onboarding
- New client trigger on CRM deal won: create Notion page, Slack channel, calendar invite
- Asset collection: automated email requesting logo, brand guide, access credentials
- Auto-create Looker Studio report and share with client email
