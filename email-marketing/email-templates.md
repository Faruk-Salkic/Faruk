# Email Marketing Notes

## Welcome Sequence (5 emails)
| # | Day | Goal | CTA |
|---|-----|------|-----|
| 1 | 0 | Set expectations, deliver lead magnet | Download |
| 2 | 2 | Build trust, humanise brand | Reply with your challenge |
| 3 | 4 | Educate, show expertise | Read article |
| 4 | 7 | Social proof via case study | Book call or see pricing |
| 5 | 10 | Soft offer | Main CTA |

## Subject Line Formulas
- Curiosity: The thing nobody tells you about [X]
- Urgency: Last chance, [offer] ends tonight
- Personal: Quick question, [First Name]
- List: 7 ways to [outcome]
- Negative frame: Why [common belief] is wrong

## Deliverability Checklist
- [ ] SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured
- [ ] Domain warm-up complete
- [ ] Remove hard bounces monthly
- [ ] Plain-text version included
- [ ] Unsubscribe link present and working
