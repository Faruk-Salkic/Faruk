# Email Automation Map

## Always-On Automations

### Welcome Series
- Trigger: New opt-in
- Emails: 5 over 10 days
- Goal: Educate and convert to first action

### Lead Magnet Delivery
- Trigger: Specific form submit
- Email 1 (immediate): Deliver resource
- Email 2 (day 3): Did you get value?
- Email 3 (day 7): Relevant offer

### Post-Purchase Follow-Up
- Trigger: Deal marked Won in CRM
- Email 1 (day 1): Welcome and what to expect
- Email 2 (day 7): Check-in
- Email 3 (day 30): Review request

### Re-engagement
- Trigger: No open in 60 days
- Email 1: Useful content with soft CTA
- Email 2 (day 5): Last chance — stay or unsubscribe
- If no open: unsubscribe automatically
