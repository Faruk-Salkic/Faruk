# Quality Score Improvement Guide

## Three Factors (1-10)
1. Expected CTR — most important
2. Ad relevance
3. Landing page experience

## Improving Expected CTR
- Test multiple headlines and pin the winner
- Use numbers and specifics: Save 40%, In 24h, From 49 EUR
- Urgency and specificity outperform generic copy

## Improving Ad Relevance
- Max 10-15 keywords per ad group (tight themes)
- Match ad copy closely to keyword intent
- Keyword in H1 and D1 minimum

## Improving Landing Page Score
- Keyword in page title and H1
- Page loads under 3s on mobile
- Clear single CTA above fold
- Message match: ad promise = page delivery

## QS Impact on Cost
| QS | Cost vs Average |
|----|----------------|
| 10 | Up to 50% cheaper |
| 7-9 | 10-30% cheaper |
| 5-6 | Average |
| 1-4 | 25-400% more expensive |
