# Bidding Strategies Cheat Sheet

## Smart Bidding Options
| Strategy | Use When | Goal |
|----------|----------|------|
| Target CPA | 30+ conversions/month | Lead gen |
| Target ROAS | Ecommerce, varied order values | Revenue |
| Maximize Conversions | New campaigns | Volume |
| Enhanced CPC | Manual control needed | Hybrid |
| Target Impression Share | Brand awareness | Visibility |

## When to Switch
- New campaign: start with Maximize Conversions
- After 30+ conversions/month: move to Target CPA
- Brand campaigns: Target Impression Share (top of page)
- ROAS tanking: check attribution model before changing strategy

## Common Mistakes
- Setting Target CPA too low too fast — needs 2-3 week learning period
- Switching strategy too often — algorithm needs time
- Not excluding brand from non-brand campaigns
- Forgetting device and location bid adjustments
