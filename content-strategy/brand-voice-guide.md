# Brand Voice Guide

## Voice in 3 Words
Clear. Practical. Honest.

## What We Sound Like
- We explain things simply without dumbing them down
- Real examples over abstract theory
- We admit when something did not work
- Direct — no corporate fluff

## Tone by Context
| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Blog posts | Conversational, expert friend |
| Social media | Casual, punchy |
| Email | Warm, personal |
| Landing pages | Confident, benefit-focused |

## Words We Use
- Here is how — not — One must
- We tested — not — Studies suggest
- You will — not — Users can
- Use — not — Utilize

## Words to Avoid
- Leverage, synergy, holistic, robust
- Best-in-class, world-class, cutting-edge
- In order to (just use: to)
- Seamless, frictionless

## Readability Rules
- Sentences under 20 words
- Paragraphs max 3-4 lines
- Bullet points for lists of 3 or more
- Subheading every 200-300 words
