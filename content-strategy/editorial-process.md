# Editorial Process

## Step 1: Idea Validation
- Does this keyword have search volume?
- Can we realistically rank for it?
- Does it match our audience buying stage?
- Do we have unique insight to add?

## Step 2: Content Brief
- Fill out brief template before writing
- Assign to writer with brief and deadline
- Brief must include: keyword, intent, outline, competitor URLs, word count

## Step 3: First Draft
- No editing during draft phase — write first, edit later
- Flag expert-input sections with [NEEDS REVIEW]

## Step 4: Editing
- Pass 1: Structure and flow
- Pass 2: On-page SEO — keyword placement, headings, internal links
- Pass 3: Copy and tone — brand voice, grammar, readability

## Step 5: Publish and Promote
- Publish with correct meta title and description
- Add internal links from 3 existing pages
- Post snippet on LinkedIn and Instagram Stories
- Submit URL to Google Search Console for indexing

## Step 6: Track and Update
- Review performance after 3 months
- Ranking 5-15: optimise on-page elements
- Not ranking after 6 months: consolidate or full rewrite
